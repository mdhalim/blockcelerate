About

Democratized Accelerator for Blockchain Start-Ups and Existing Businesses

Blockcelerate is designed to provides mentorship and expertise to selected blockchain projects in their development, leading them towards their own successful Initial Coin Offering (ICO).

Blockcelerate seeks to become a world leading platform that will drive the next wave of blockchain innovation.

Read More

While many projects with brilliant ideas emerge every day, many of them will never get the funds they need in order to execute the project. In order to run a successful Initial Coin Offering (ICO), start-ups face a number of challenges including:

Funding the ICO Process

It is difficult tofundraise for projects in early phase, even though it is a publicly beneficial project.

Difficult to recruit Talents

It is difficult to recruit good team members at initial stage. Giving stock options (vesting and cliff) may be one option, but it is local expensive and a slow process.

Lack of Marketing Expertise

It is crucial to get the marketing aspects right, as a sucessful ICO is most reliant on the community that contributes and supports the project.

Lack of Knowledge on ICO Process

Teams may have great ideas but lack the knowledge, and nessary advise on structuring and running a successful ICO.

Blockelerate seeks to provide Start-ups with the best chance of success by providing access to:

Smart Contract Auditors

Smart contract writers and auditors are in high demand these days. Through the BCL Platform, Our users will have access to the best smart contract developers and auditors.

Cmmunity Managers

Community through channels such as Telegram, Slack, Reddit and Medium. The community plays a vital role in a successful ICO.

Marketing & PR

Well known blockchain marketers know exacitly what to do with improving the involved with building visiblity leading up to the ICO

Blockchain Developers

Some of the best blockchain developers may be found across the globe and geographical factors may restrict access to these developers. Through the BCL Platform, We are creating a global economy for projects to access the best developers and to hire them using the BCL Tokens.

Advisors

Having the right advisiors can go a long way in helping a project gain visiblity and credibility. The right advisiors will also be able to provide teams with an experienced supporting cast to learn from.

Blockchain Legal Specialists

The ICO Landscape and regulations and constantly changing and having access to legal Experts in this field can be of tremendous help to a new project. They can also be paid in issued tokens.
