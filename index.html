About

Democratized Accelerator for Blockchain Start-Ups and Existing Businesses

Blockcelerate is designed to provide mentorship and expertise to selected blockchain projects in their development, leading them towards their own successful Initial Coin Offering (ICO).

Blockcelerate seeks to become a world leading platform that will drive the next wave of blockchain innovation.

The Challenge

While many projects with brilliant ideas emerge every day, many of them will never get the funds they need in order to execute the project. In order to run a successful Initial Coin Offering (ICO), start-ups face a number of challenges including:

Difficult to Raise Funds

The entire ICO process can be a costly endeavour, and start-ups may face challenges in securing seed funding in the early stage.

Difficult to Recruit Talent

It can be very difficult to recruit good team members, especially at early stage. Providing stock options may not be sufficient to incentivise the best Talents as early stage equities are illiquid.

Lack of Marketing Expertise

It is crucial to get the marketing aspects right, as a sucessful ICO is most reliant on the community that contributes and supports the project.

Lack of Knowledge on ICO Process

Teams may have great ideas but lack the knowledge, and nessary advice on structuring and running a successful ICO.

The Solution

Blockcelerate seeks to provide start-ups with the best chance of success by providing access to:

Smart Contract Auditors

Smart contract writers and auditors are in high demand these days. Through the BCL Platform, our users will have access to the best smart contract developers and auditors.

Community Managers

The community plays a vital role in a successful ICO. Our experienced Community Managers on the BCL Platform will assist with community building and management through channels such as Telegram, Slack, Reddit and Medium.

Marketing & PR

Well-known blockchain marketers know exactly what to do to improve the marketability of tokens. They will be involved with building visiblity leading up to the ICO

Blockchain Developers

Some of the best blockchain developers may be found across the globe and geographical factors may restrict access to these developers. Through the BCL Platform, we are creating a global economy for projects to access the best developers and to hire them wherever they may be.

Advisors

Having the right advisiors can go a long way in helping a project gain visiblity and credibility. The right advisors will also be able to provide teams with an experienced supporting cast to learn from.

Blockchain Legal Specialists

The ICO landscape and regulations are constantly changing and having access to legal experts in this field is vital to a new project
